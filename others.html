Things To Note

HOW TO GET XPs

Xps can be gotten from wining battles

Beating an opponent in your rank or below yours - 1xp
Beating an opponent 2 ranks above you - 2xp
Beating an opponent before or at 5 turns - +1xp

Xps can also be bought from an admin at the rate of 5xp per 100naira(bonus will be given)
Note: Crowns cannot be bought

ALL BATTLE TYPES

1-on-1 Battle
2-on-2 Tag Team Battle
2-on-1 Handicap Battle
Fusion Battle
Tournament Battle
Event Battle
Beast Battle
Death Battle

During Event & Beast Battles, you are allowed to use special gains called Senzu Beans & Energy capsules. During eventmbattles you can use 2 of each while during beast battles you can only use 1 of each. To use these gains they must be bought before the battle. When you use these gains in the battle, you still play.

Senzu Bean: Gain 5 health. 1xp to buy.
Energy Capsule: Gain 5cp. 1xp to buy.

CHARACTER CARD

A new character card can be bought for 40xp. This card must be from another race. You can switch between character cards to use during battles but cannot switch during a battle(unless you have a skill which can do that).

HOW TO GET CROWNS

Crowns are also gotten by battling

Beating an opponent in your rank or below yours - 30 crowns
Beating an opponent 2 ranks above you - 40 crowns
Beating an opponent before or at 5 turns - +10 crowns

Note: You can run from a battle without losing crowns by using 1xp.
